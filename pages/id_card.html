Project Description

Purpose: This project was created for my college to provide a secure digital system for managing student identity and academic information, addressing the issue of temporary or unavailable result data.

Problem Statement: In our college, marks and results are only displayed for a specific period. Students often do not receive marksheets quickly. Once the data is deleted or unavailable, students cannot access their past academic details.

Solution: The system provides a digital ID card and student information management platform that securely stores student records. Features like QR code scanning, complaint management, and access to personal and academic details digitally enhance college administration.

Key Features:

✅ Digital College ID Card ✅ QR Code for Student Details ✅ Complaint & Safety System ✅ Secure Login for Personal & Education Pages ✅ Access Semester Results Digitally ✅ Download Bus Pass, ID Card, Bonafide Certificate ✅ Staff Approval for Digital Documents ✅ Centralized Record Management

Importance: Prevents loss of academic data, improves student safety, and enables quick access to digital certificates and ID cards. Students can access personal and education information securely, ensuring efficiency and reliability.

Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, QR Code Integration, Secure Login Concepts, Responsive Web Design.

Conclusion: This project provides a secure, efficient, and digital solution for managing student identity, academic records, and college services. It ensures that students can access their information even if official data is unavailable, improving college administration and digital transformation.
